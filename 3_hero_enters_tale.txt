The princess cried all night by the lake. In the early morning, as the sun was rising, she saw a shining frog by the shore. 
"Let's just try  one last frog", she thought. So she gently took it by the hand and kissed it. Nothing happened. 
A tear was coming from her eye when, suddenly, the frog jumped and exploded in a magic cloud. When the cloud vanished there he was: 
a handsome prince and his sidekick, a small white cat.
